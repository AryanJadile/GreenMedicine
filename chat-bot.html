What are the best dietary choices for promoting healthy aging?

 

The best dietary choices for healthy aging include a balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, whole grains, lean proteins, and healthy fats. Limiting processed foods, added sugars, and excessive salt can help reduce the risk of chronic diseases. Staying hydrated and getting essential nutrients like calcium and vitamin D is also crucial for bone health.
